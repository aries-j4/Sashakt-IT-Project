About Us

Various types of crimes that take place against women such as eve-teasing, harassment, domestic violence, etc. are a cause of major concern for the society and it is crucial that immediate and effective help be provided to victims of such crimes.

‘Sashakt’ is an initiative towards the cause of women safety and security that will provide support to victims be it mental or physical and also help spread awareness about such issues to help empower women.
